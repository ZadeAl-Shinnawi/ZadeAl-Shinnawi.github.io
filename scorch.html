Scorch Game Engine

Scorch, built using C++ and OpenGL, is an in-progress game engine. It is intended to showcase a myriad of advanced techniques to deliver stunning visual experiences and harness the full potential to achieve real-time rendering of complex scenes, including dynamic lighting, realistic shadows, and high-quality textures. It is in the very early stages of development while I complete my senior year of college.
